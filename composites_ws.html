Composites Workshop

Workshop description:

This workshop is to train students on the basic fabrication of a glass epoxy laminate by hand layup, cured by compression molding, and to introduce to them the procedures involved in testing the laminates for tensile, compression and flexural tests. The students will also be introduced to impact strength determination and dynamic mechanical analysis.

Workshop Format:
  • Introductory lecture
  • Fabrication of laminates by hand layup
  • Testing of laminates on Universal Testing Machine
  • Laboratory tour to introduce Impact testing and dynamic mechanical analysis
  • Lecture on future trends in composites for the aerospace industry
  • Students quiz
