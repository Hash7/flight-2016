CAD Modelling

Drawings and diagrams are the best communication tools for engineers. If you have the passion for designing, this is the right place for you. This FLIGHT 2k16 will bring you an exclusive CAD based design event.

Event description:

The basics of modelling will be tested in the first round after which in the consecutive rounds, the participants are required to complete designs using CATIA V5. The fastest designer with most accuracy of the given problem will be declared winner.

Event format:
  • There will be two rounds.
  • First round is a preliminary round consisting of a written test on basics of Computer Aided Design and Engineering Graphics.
  • Candidates shortlisted for the second and final round will be required to complete the design using CATIA V5 as per the problem statement given on the spot.
  • Participants will be given a time of 60 minutes to complete their CAD models.
