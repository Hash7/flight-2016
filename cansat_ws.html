CAN SATELLITE (CANSAT)

Workshop description:

A CanSat is a simulation of a real satellite, integrated within the volume and shape of a aerodynamic box. The challenge for the students is to fit all the major subsystems found in a satellite, such as power, sensors and a communication system, into this minimal volume.

Workshop format:

Theory class about Understanding Rocket and its flight Dynamics.
  • Design and Fabrication of Rocket and its frame.
  • Design and fabrication of parachute.
  • Design and assembly of payload (Arduino, Sensors, PCB..,).
  • Programming to onboard computer.
  • Testing and calibration of sensors.
  • Launching of satellite.
