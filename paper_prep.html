Paper Presentation
Event description:

How well you know your basics is reflected in how well you can articulate your ideas in a short time. Come and share your ideas to better the future of the aviation industry with us.
A technical paper would be a research paper or simply a review paper based on a review of present technologies or technology that would be possible in near future.

Event format:
  • The event consists of two rounds.
  • First round will be online submission of abstract. Abstracts should be mailed to aero_association@mitindia.edu or mitflight16@gmail.com on or before March 8 with subject as “Flight 16 Paper presentation”.
  • Abstracts should not exceed 500 words. It should be typed in MS Word format, Times New Roman, Font Size 12.
  • Shortlisted candidates will be intimated by mail on or before March 10.
  • They must present their ideas on March 11 at 1 p.m.
Rules:
  • Maximum of two per team.
  • The students must bring their college ID card without fail.
  • Each team will be given 15 minutes. 10 minutes for the presenting their paper and 5 minutes will be for questions from judges and the audience.
  • Number of slides= 12 to 15. The presentation should be brought in a USB.
  • Judges’ decision is final.
