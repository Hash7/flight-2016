RC fixed wing workshop

Workshop description:

A workshop on Flight controller integration and is about designing, developing and integrating a flight controller for a UAV. Workshop focuses on microcontroller selection together with a barometer, gyroscope, accelerometer and GPS (optional) integration to a flight controller system which can perform semi-autonomous or fully autonomous flight required for an unmanned aerial vehicle.
