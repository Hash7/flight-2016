JET Workshop

Workshop description:

A lecture and an animative presentation on various flow visualization techniques will be conducted. Flow visualization is the art of making flow patterns visible and the main focus of this workshop is to visualize jet flows by the use of Schlieren technique. Schlieren visualization shows a jet flow in light and dark patterns according to the expansion and compression properties of the flow, which will be explained in detail here.

Workshop format:
  • First session is lecture
  • Second session is flow visualization of a jet flow in laboratory
